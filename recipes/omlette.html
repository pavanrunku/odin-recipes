Egglicious Omlette

Description

Eggs can liven up a meal in many ways, served in different styles all over the world. In Malaysia, eggs have been served creatively in many ways, accompanying classic meals beloved by many now. For instance, we have egg sambal, which combines the delicious heaty kick of a spicy sauteed paste with sliced hard-boiled eggs. Leftover eggs cooked for nasi lemak or earned from weddings are not wasted if you whip up this recipe to be eaten with rice the next day. Some home cooks also love to include eggs in dishes with vegetables, such as asparagus with salted eggs or stir-fried long beans with eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs- 4
  • Shrimps - 50g
  • Spring Onion - 2 sprig
  • Red chili - 1
  • Tomato - 1
  • MAGGI CukupRasa - 1 tbsp
  • Cooking Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Coriander - 1 sprig

Instructions

Step1

In a medium mixing bowl, crack the eggs open. Next, add in the shrimps, spring onion, red chilli, tomato and MAGGI CukupRasa. Beat well with a fork. Set it aside.

Step2

heat the cooking oil in a non-stick pan until almost smoky.

Step3

Gently pour in the egg mixture and tilt the pan to spread the egg mixture evenly.

Step4

Let the egg cook for 5-8 minutes or until it turns golden brown underneath. Then, using a spatula, flip over the egg and let the other side cook.

Step5

Get a serving plate and tip the omelette gently until it slides off the pan and onto the plate. Garnish with shredded spring onion and coriander before serving.
