Maggi Recipe

Description

Maggi Anda Bhurji is an Indian fusion recipe of Maggi noodles and scrambled eggs. It is an easy-to-make, simple, and delicious Maggi recipe that one can have as a snack or breakfast dish.

Ingredients

Ingredients to make:

  • Maggi noodles
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Onion & Tomato
  • Spices

Steps to make

  1. Step on is to boil the Maggi noodles and drain the excess water from them. Then we need to saute onion in oil until tey are soft.
  2. Now we add ginger-garlic paste and mix it. Then it is the turn for spices and tomato. Mix everything and cook until oil separates. If you add more vegetables like peas or corn, it will also be added in this step. They may take some more time to cook.
  3. Then comes the eggs. We need to break the yolk and scramble them. In other words, make bhurji. Once done, we will add the noodles and mix.
  4. Lastly, we will add the Maggi masala and mix one final time. Presto! We are done.
